Our Mission 🎯

Building bridges between communities and street dogs

🏥

Healthcare Access

Providing medical care, vaccinations, and sterilization programs for street dogs to ensure their health and well-being.

🍽️

Feeding Programs

Establishing sustainable feeding stations and organizing community feeding drives to ensure no dog goes hungry.

🏠

Shelter & Adoption

Creating safe spaces and connecting loving families with dogs ready for their forever homes.

📚

Community Education

Raising awareness about animal welfare and promoting coexistence between humans and street dogs.
